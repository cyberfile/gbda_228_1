MealMate

Project Details

Timeline Winter 2025
My Role UX Design, Product Thinking, Prototyping
Tools Figma, FigJam
Platform iOS Mobile App

Mealmate

AI Ingredient Detection & Recipe Suggestor

Let's face it—our parents buy us groceries, but using them efficiently isn't always easy. To solve this, we designed a smart ingredient-detecting camera that generates personalized recipes based on difficulty and prep time.

Guided Cooking

Once you select a recipe, you can follow along through both video and text, complete with clear measurement breakdowns for every ingredient.

Community

After cooking, you can upload a photo of your dish for your friends and university community to see. It's a space to celebrate growth, cheer each other on, and build connections through food.

Lessons Learned

Building MealMate taught us that even simple daily decisions—like what to cook—can feel overwhelming for students navigating independence for the first time. By blending AI, community, and accessible design, we learned how powerful it can be to turn everyday challenges into opportunities for connection and confidence.

Visipal

A Chrome extension that helps visually impaired students explore the web with clarity, confidence, and ease.

Project Details

Timeline 7 hours (2025)
My Role Product Ideation, Research, Product Designer
Tools Figma
Platform Chrome Extension (Web)

Project Overview

UW/UX Flow Designathon / First Place
Timeline: 7 hours (2025)
My Role: Product Ideation, Research, Product Designer

As the person in charge of product ideation, I chose to explore a Chrome extension because it improves an existing experience rather than creating a completely new one. While researching accessibility tools, I noticed that many older reading aids lacked customization and didn't support the specific needs of children with visual impairments. That insight shaped the direction of Visipal.

User Persona

Alex is an energetic 8-year-old who's endlessly curious—especially about snakes, animals, and anything he can read about online. But because he has nystagmus and low vision, the digital world isn't always built for him.

What Alex truly wants is the ability to read with ease, explore topics he loves, and learn at the same pace as his peers. He's eager, motivated, and capable—he just needs a digital environment that meets him where he is.

Emphasis on Customization in Onboarding

The onboarding flow helps each user personalize VisiPal to their specific visual needs in four simple steps — from choosing support type, to text settings, colour themes, and smart learning tools like AI summaries and Focus Mode.

Before VisiPal

After VisiPal

VisiPods

VisiPods is a shared learning hub where students can post what they're discovering, celebrate their curiosity, and feel empowered by seeing how much they're learning. It creates a supportive space where kids can explore together.

Lessons Learned

VisiPal showed me how meaningful it is to design for real, specific needs—especially for kids like Alex, who are curious, eager, and often overlooked in digital accessibility. Ultimately, VisiPal is a reminder that empathetic, intentional design doesn't just solve problems—it opens doors.

Overlap

Overlap is a remote-work communication platform that strengthens team connection, streamlines cross–time zone collaboration, and supports smoother onboarding for faster, clearer project alignment.

Project Details
Category Detail
Project Overlap — Remote Work Communication Platform
Award GBDA Soc Innovation Challenge — Second Place
Timeline 7 hours (2025)
My Role Product Thinking, Business Modelling
Tools Figma, FigJam
Platform Desktop / Mobile Web App

Project Overview

GBDA Soc Innovation Challenge / Second Place
Timeline: 7 hours (2025)
My Role: Product Thinking, Business Modelling

As a first-year university student, I experienced firsthand how overwhelming it can be to navigate remote collaboration across time zones. How can we make remote work feel less lonely and more connected—no matter where you are?

Onboarding

Overlap includes a connection-focused onboarding flow that goes beyond job titles. Instead of only collecting your role and department, it invites you to share a short personal bio and select your interests. This information appears on your profile, giving teammates an easy way to connect with you on a more human level.

Hangouts

The Meet feature helps remote teammates build meaningful connections beyond their usual circles. Overlap pairs you with a coworker for a casual Hangout based on your availability, preferred frequency, and shared interests. It's a simple way to spark authentic relationships and strengthen team culture, no matter the distance.

Activity and Rooms

The Activity tab makes it easy to stay aligned, even if you missed a meeting. You can review meeting summaries, check who attended, revisit the taskboard, and browse any polls that were shared. Within Rooms, explore each department's workspace with shared community files.

Lessons Learned

Working on Overlap taught me how important it is to design tools that balance efficiency with genuine human connection. Remote teams don't just need clearer communication—they need spaces that help them understand each other, build trust, and collaborate confidently across time zones.

EcoBloom

EcoBloom is a gamified sustainability app that motivates users to build better environmental habits through challenges, rewards, and a growing virtual garden.

Project Details
Category Detail
Project EcoBloom — Gamified Sustainability App
Award UW/UX Fuse Designathon — UW/UX Pick
Timeline 8 hours (2024)
My Role Product Designer, User Researcher
Tools Figma, FigJam
Platform iOS Mobile App

Project Overview

UW/UX Fuse Designathon / UW/UX Pick
Timeline: 8 hours (2024)
My Role: Product Designer, User Researcher

EcoBloom reimagines sustainable habits as a fun, community-driven challenge for students. The app tracks everyday eco-friendly actions and turns them into points that contribute to both personal progress and friendly competition. With school and interschool leaderboards, students can see their collective impact and stay motivated through shared goals.

User Persona

Habit Tracker

EcoBloom turns sustainable habits into a rewarding, gamified experience. Each eco-friendly action you complete earns positive feedback and moves you closer to measurable progress. Every task contributes to growing a new plant in your personal garden.

Leaderboard

EcoBloom's leaderboards bring sustainability to life at both the school and interschool level. You can see how your community ranks, explore the eco-friendly actions students are taking, and feel motivated by the collective impact.

Lessons Learned

Working on EcoBloom showed me how powerful gamification can be when it's paired with real-world impact. I learned that students are far more motivated when sustainability feels fun, visual, and community-driven. Ultimately, this project reinforced that encouraging environmental change isn't just about tracking behavior—it's about sparking pride, connection, and a sense of belonging around a common goal.
